About Us

Crazy Cookies Bakery is a family owned and operated business and has a long standing history of distributing ingredients of only the highest quality, with unparalleled service standards. We started out in Turkey, then our family moved to Canada in 2014, and then our first kiosk was opened in June 2015; it offers a homestyle bakery experience in Hyde Park/London area. Crazy Cookies Bakery is about a sense of family and coming together through food, that is why we consider our customer and employees to be family. We strive to serve the highest quality and variety of imported and local foods.
